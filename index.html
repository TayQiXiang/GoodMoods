Why GoodMoods ?

n this modern era, it is important to provide online telehealth platforms like websites to help individuals recognise mental health difficulties and start online therapy with professionals.

  • Some researchers have found that online therapy is as effective in reducing stress as having face-to-face therapy with psychologists.
  • To quickly identify people with potential mental illness
  • To help people with mental health issues find a mental health professional

What are the problems ?

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, students and workers tend to have mental illnesses as they are not allowed to work or study on-site and this leads to a lack of communication with others to express feelings.

  1. Those who have a mental health concern are unable to manage stress in an effective manner as they are not exposed to an online platform to learn to cope with an emotional disorder.

  2. Most of the students and workers lack knowledge of mental health conditions and they do not realize the importance of maintaining mental well-being.

  3. Individuals, especially students who are unable to afford counselling fees cannot attend the counselling sessions provided by professionals.

What can we offer ?

We aim to reduce mental illness, using several features below

  • Provide screening tests for end-users to understand their mental well-being
  • Booking of Counselling session
  • Provides videos to promote mental well-being
  • Email notifications on a weekly basis
  • Provide a platform to share your thoughts and feelings
  • And many more coming...
